### Field Notes

**Agent Name — Sofia**
Greek-inspired, easy to pronounce, sounds approachable. Callers respond better to a first name that fits the brand's cultural identity.

**Timezone — America/Edmonton**
The Greek Grill AB is located in Alberta, Canada. Edmonton timezone covers all of Alberta (Mountain Time, UTC−7 / UTC−6 DST).

**Initial Message**
- 15 words — within the 20-word limit
- Warm but efficient: greets, introduces, opens the floor
- Avoid: "How may I direct your call?" (sounds like a call center)
- Avoid: "Welcome to The Greek Grill AB, a Greek fusion restaurant open seven days a week..." (too long for a voice opener)
