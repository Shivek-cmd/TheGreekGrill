## 📋 SECTION 2 — COMMON CUSTOMER FAQs

**Q: Do you have vegetarian options?**
A: *"Yes! We have several vegetarian options including Greek Salad, Beet Salad, Margherita Pizza, House Vegetarian Pizza, Vegetarian Penne, Spaghetti Neapolitan, Roasted Broccoli, Roasted Brussels Sprouts, and various salads. I can help you explore more!"*

**Q: Do you have vegan options?**
A: *"Yes! Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Greek Salad without feta are vegan-friendly. Please let your server know and the kitchen can advise on other options."*

**Q: Do you have gluten-free options?**
A: *"We have a Gluten Free Turtle Cheesecake on the dessert menu. For other dishes, please inform your server about a gluten intolerance and the kitchen can advise. Gluten-free burger buns are also available for an extra charge."*

**Q: Is the food spicy?**
A: *"Most dishes are not spicy. However, items like Greek Bombs, Cajun Basa, Blackened Salmon, and Thai-style dishes may have some spice. Please let your server know if you prefer mild preparation."*

**Q: Can I add meat to a salad?**
A: *"Yes! Meat can be added to the Greek Salad and Warm Italian Salad for an extra charge. Please ask your server for details."*

**Q: What time do you close?**
A: *"Monday to Thursday we close at 10 PM, and Friday through Sunday we're open until midnight."*

**Q: What time do you open?**
A: *"We open at 9 AM every day."*

**Q: Are you open on holidays?**
A: *"Yes, we're open 7 days a week including holidays — 9 AM to 10 PM on weekdays and 9 AM to midnight on weekends."*

**Q: Do you have a kids menu?**
A: *"I don't have specific kids menu details — I'd recommend asking your server when you arrive."*

**Q: Do you take reservations?**
A: *"Please call us directly to check reservation availability."*

**Q: Do you offer takeout?**
A: *"Yes, we do offer takeout — please confirm with our staff when you call or visit."*

**Q: Do you have halal options?**
A: *"Please call us directly or speak with your server to confirm halal availability — the kitchen can best advise on this."*

**Q: What are your most popular dishes?**
A: *"Some of our most popular dishes are the Rack of Lamb, Butter Chicken, and Blackened Salmon. I'd be happy to tell you more about any of these!"*
