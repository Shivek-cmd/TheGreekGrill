# The Greek Grill AB — GHL Knowledge Base Split Guide
> **Purpose:** Structure for splitting the full menu KB into 5 GHL-compatible knowledge bases
> **Character Limit per KB:** ~25,000 characters
> **Total KBs:** 5

---

## Overview — Why 5 Knowledge Bases?

The full knowledge base exceeds GHL's 25,000-character limit and covers very different types of customer questions. Splitting by topic lets the voice agent retrieve **precise answers faster** without searching irrelevant content.

KB 4 was split into two separate KBs so the agent never confuses a drink order with a restaurant policy question.

---

## KB 1 — Starters, Snacks & Light Bites

**Suggested GHL Name:** `GG_KB1_Starters_And_Snacks`

**Description:**
Includes appetizers, finger foods, soups, salads, and light meals at The Greek Grill AB—ideal for starters, sharing plates, or lighter dining options before mains.

**When the Voice Agent Should Use This KB:**
- Customer asks about appetizers, starters, or "something to share"
- Customer mentions: wings, calamari, spring rolls, ribs, fries, bruschetta, arancini, scallops (starter), shrimp (starter), pita bombs, broccoli, brussels sprouts, garlic bread, or potato skins
- Customer asks about **soups** (daily soup menu)
- Customer asks about **salads** (Greek salad, Caesar, beet, fennel, tuna, seafood, Thai shrimp, etc.)
- Customer asks about **lighter options**, **vegetarian starters**, or **vegan-friendly** dishes
- Customer asks about allergens in starters or salads

## KB 5 — Restaurant Info & FAQs

**Suggested GHL Name:** `GG_KB5_Restaurant_Info`

**Description:**
Covers all general restaurant information for The Greek Grill AB — hours, reservations, takeout, dietary options, allergen policy, halal, kids menu, popular dishes, and a food glossary.

**When the Voice Agent Should Use This KB:**
- Customer asks about **opening hours**, **closing time**, or **days open**
- Customer asks about **reservations** or **takeout**
- Customer asks **general questions**: vegetarian options, vegan options, gluten-free options, spice levels, kids menu, halal, allergen policy
- Customer needs to know if the restaurant is **open on holidays**
- Customer asks what a food term means (souvlaki, tzatziki, arancini, etc.)
- Customer asks what the **most popular dishes** are
- Customer asks a question that does **not fit** KB 1, 2, 3, or 4
